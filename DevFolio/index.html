About me

I am a Mechanical Engineering student interested in manufacturing, robotics, rapid prototyping, and building physical systems that connect design, code, and real-world testing.

I like projects that move from screen to shop: modeling parts, writing control code, fabricating prototypes, testing hardware, and iterating until the system actually works.
