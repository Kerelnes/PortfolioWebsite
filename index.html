Interests

Areas I keep coming back to: building, testing, manufacturing, and making physical systems smarter.

Robotics & Mechatronics

Systems that combine mechanical design, sensors, motors, and code to create useful physical behavior.

Embedded Systems

Manufacturing

CNC, 3D printing, laser cutting, machining constraints, tolerances, and designing parts that can actually be made.

Rapid Prototyping
